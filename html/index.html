An Introduction to Electronics

Begin by reading Is this course for me?, and if it is, then start off by reading the Gentle introduction, which should lead you nicely to The progressive guide. Or, if you feel comfortable, jump straight to one of the topics below.

If you want to get a feel for where this will take you, have a look at the later exercises, such as building a radio or An optical fiber telephone.
